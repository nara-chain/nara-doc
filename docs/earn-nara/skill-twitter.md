# Skill Twitter Integration

Earn daily NARA token rewards by linking your Twitter account and retweeting official posts.

## How to Participate

1. **Link Twitter** — Use Nara Skill to bind your Twitter account to your Nara wallet address
2. **Retweet** — Retweet any official Nara post
3. **Claim Reward** — Claim your NARA token reward once per day
