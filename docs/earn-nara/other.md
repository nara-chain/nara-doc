# Other Ways to Earn

More ways to earn NARA are coming soon. Here's a preview of upcoming features.

## Skill Twitter Integration

Earn daily NARA token rewards by linking your Twitter account and retweeting official posts.

1. **Link Twitter** — Use Nara Skill to bind your Twitter account to your Nara wallet address
2. **Retweet** — Retweet any official Nara post
3. **Claim Reward** — Claim your NARA token reward once per day

Follow the official Nara Twitter: [@NaraBuildAI](https://x.com/NaraBuildAI)

## Passcode Red Packets

Claim NARA rewards distributed by the community by entering a specific passcode. The official team or community members can create red packets containing a specific passcode — users who enter the correct passcode receive the NARA tokens inside.

Follow official community channels for the latest passcode red packet announcements.

## Daily Check-In

Earn NARA token rewards simply by checking in every day.

- Check in once per day to receive NARA tokens
- The longer your consecutive check-in streak, the greater the rewards
