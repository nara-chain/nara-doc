# Passcode Red Packets

Claim NARA rewards distributed by the community by entering a specific passcode.

## What Are Passcode Red Packets

Passcode red packets are a community engagement mechanism. The official team or community members can create red packets containing a specific passcode. Users who enter the correct passcode receive the NARA tokens inside.

## How to Claim

Follow official community channels for the latest passcode red packet announcements.
