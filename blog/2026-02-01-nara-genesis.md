# Nara Chain Is Live

We are excited to announce that **Nara Chain** has officially completed its genesis launch.

Nara Chain is a new Layer 1 blockchain forked from Solana, inheriting Solana's high-performance architecture while introducing new directions for innovation.

<!-- truncate -->

## Why Nara

Solana stands out in the blockchain space with its exceptional performance — sub-second block times, extremely low transaction fees, and a parallel execution engine. However, a mature ecosystem also means slower innovation cycles and higher costs for experimentation.

Nara was created to explore new possibilities while building on Solana's technical strengths. We believe a lean, agile chain can experiment and validate new ideas faster.

## What Was Accomplished at Genesis

### Ecosystem Migration

In the genesis block, we deployed a curated selection of Solana's core ecosystem protocols:

- **Token Standards** — SPL Token, Token-2022, Associated Token Account
- **NFT Ecosystem** — Metaplex Metadata, Bubblegum (cNFT), Core
- **DeFi Infrastructure** — Full Meteora protocol suite (DLMM, DAMM v2, etc.)
- **Multisig Tools** — Squads v3/v4

This means developers can start building on Nara immediately without waiting for an ecosystem to be built from scratch.

### Native Programs

Beyond the migrated ecosystem, we deployed Nara's own on-chain programs:

- **Quest** — PoMI (Proof of Machine Intelligence) system
- **Skill** — AI Agent capability registration and invocation
- **Domain** — On-chain domain name system
- **MCP** — Multi-Call Protocol

## Solana Compatibility

Nara is fully compatible with Solana's toolchain. Your Solana wallet keys work directly on Nara, and `@solana/web3.js` can connect to Nara's RPC for development.

## Roadmap

- Continuous synchronization with the Agave validator client
- Independent improvement proposal process
- Expansion of the PoMI and Skill ecosystems
- NARA airdrop to all SOL holders
